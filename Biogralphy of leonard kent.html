Dr Leonard kent belives in proving to be a model for his patients so that they adapt a healthy lifestyle. Patient with wide variety of musculoskeletal injuries come to him. He helps his patients get back to whatever they enjoy doing by treating their orthopedic problems like cartilage damage,complex hip & shoulder problems,knee injuries and sprains.

Dr Leonard Kent also performs minimally invasive surgeries to reduce pain and ensure shorter hospital stays This approach help the patients quickly recover and get back to their normal healthy lives He relies on a comprehensive approach of caring for his patients by using the latest diagnostic tools and pain management regimes
